Roasted Brussel Sprouts

Overview

While most people view roasted vegetables as a fall or winter dish, these roasted brussel sprouts pack a savory punch while bringing a fresh pop of spice and brightness. Incredibly simple to make, these delicious sprouts will have even the pickiest eaters itching to try them. As an added bonus, your kitchen will smell absolutely delightful.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 lbs. Fresh Brussel Sprouts
  • 3 Tbsp Olive Oil
  • 3/4 tsp Kosher Salt
  • 1/2 tsp Fresh Ground Black Pepper
  • 1 tsp Garlic Powder
  • 1/2 tsp Red Pepper Flakes (Optional)
  • 1/2 tsp Lemon Juice (Optional)
